FINDING THE NEED IN STREAMING

At the end of Quarte 1 we were tasked to find the need in streaming. But what is Streaming?

According to Merriam Webster “streaming is an act, the process, or an instance of Streaming data”. so in live streaming, it is an act of recording yourself live on a platform, data being transferred, which can be referred to as the video being streamed to you at an instance.
